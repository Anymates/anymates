Any Shake is a camera shake effect that you can customise and animate to your needs. We created this tool for video editors with the goal of keeping it simple and efficient, so you can save time and get the result you need quickly.

Simple by design

No more dozens of presets that often don’t behave as expected. Any Shake is drag-and-drop and simple to configure.

Integrated features

Any Shake includes motion blur and a mirror edges mode to hide black bars caused by the clip's movement.

One seed, seamless editing

Using the same seed creates a continuous transition with no visible cut between clips.

We aim to keep improving this shake plugin and adding new features. Your feedback and suggestions are welcome to help us in this process.

Known issues

Scale down in Motion creates cropping.

To solve this issue, use a Transform effect before Any Shake to scale down your clip.

Scale up with a Transform effect before Any Shake shifts and crops your clip.

To fix this, use the Motion scale parameter to scale up.

The Mirror Edges function may create small gaps at the edges.

As the gaps are transparent, duplicating the clip, scaling it up slightly, and putting it bellow the original clip will hide the gaps.

When animating a parameters on Adjustment layer, the first keyframe is taken into account and the others are ignored.

To fix this, use a nest to apply the effect to severals clips.
